# Scope Definition - V1

# Features Included in V1

## 1. Unified Dashboard

Displays:
- impressions
- clicks
- conversions
- spend
- CTR
- ROAS

across multiple platforms.

---

## 2. Cross-Channel Comparison

Allows analysts to compare marketing performance between channels.

Example:
- Google Ads
- Meta Ads
- LinkedIn Ads

---

## 3. Automated Insights

The system generates simple rule-based insights such as:

- "Google Ads generated the highest ROAS this week."
- "Meta campaign CTR decreased by 12%."

---

## 4. Date Filters

Users can select:
- today
- last 7 days
- last 30 days

---

## 5. Report Export

Export reports as:
- CSV
- PDF

---

# Features Excluded from V1

## AI Prediction Engine

Reason:
Too complex for first release.

---

## Real-Time Streaming Data

Reason:
Not necessary for initial product validation.

---

## Campaign Editing

Reason:
The tool is designed for analytics, not campaign management.

---

## Full Client Portal

Reason:
Internal analyst workflow is higher priority.

---

## Advanced Dashboard Customization

Reason:
Could increase development complexity without immediate value.

---

# Why This Scope Was Chosen

The goal of V1 is to create a simple but genuinely useful product.

The focus is:
- usability,
- speed,
- consistency,
- and reducing manual effort.

A smaller focused scope reduces engineering complexity and improves delivery speed.
