# Product Walkthrough

This project focuses on solving a real operational problem faced by marketing analysts.

The current workflow requires analysts to manually collect data from multiple marketing tools and prepare reports manually. This creates delays, inconsistencies, and dependency on experienced team members.

I chose internal analysts as the primary users because they are the people most affected by the current process.

The product is intentionally scoped as a lightweight internal dashboard instead of a large enterprise platform. The goal is to provide fast value with minimal complexity.

Several advanced features such as predictive AI, real-time analytics, and client self-service portals were intentionally excluded from V1. Although valuable, these features would increase development complexity and reduce focus during the initial release.

The proposed system integrates with existing marketing platforms through APIs and centralizes campaign metrics into a unified dashboard.

The key design priorities were:
- simplicity,
- usability,
- reliability,
- and reduced manual effort.

If given more time, future improvements would include:
- AI-generated recommendations,
- anomaly detection,
- predictive analytics,
- and automated alerts.
