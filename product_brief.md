# Product Brief

## Product Name

Marketing Performance Insights Dashboard

---

# Background

Marketing analysts currently use multiple tools to understand campaign performance.

To answer simple questions like:

> "How is our marketing performing across channels right now, and where should we focus?"

team members manually collect data from multiple platforms and create reports themselves.

This process is:
- repetitive,
- slow,
- inconsistent,
- and dependent on experienced analysts.

---

# Objective

Design an internal tool that centralizes marketing performance data and helps analysts quickly generate actionable insights.

---

# Primary User

Internal Marketing Analysts

These users:
- prepare reports,
- analyze campaign performance,
- and communicate insights to clients.

---

# Secondary Users

- Account Managers
- Clients (future phase)

---

# User Pain Points

1. Too much manual work
2. Reports take too long
3. Data exists in multiple tools
4. Reports differ between analysts
5. Difficult to quickly identify top-performing channels

---

# Proposed Product

A centralized dashboard that integrates multiple marketing platforms and provides:

- unified campaign metrics,
- performance comparison,
- automated summaries,
- and exportable reports.

---

# Success Criteria

The product is successful if:

- reporting time decreases,
- reporting consistency improves,
- analysts spend less time manually gathering data,
- and teams can answer client questions faster.

---

# Constraints

The current marketing tools used by the company will not change.

The solution must integrate around existing workflows and tools.
